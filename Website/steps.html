Muffin Recipe

Follow these steps to bake delicious muffins:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. Mix butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add vanilla extract and egg whites, mixing well.
  4. Stir in the buttermilk.
  5. Combine flour, baking powder, cocoa powder, and salt in another bowl.
  6. Slowly mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
  7. Fill the muffin liners with batter.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Cool for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
