Muffin Steps

Steps to follow for delicious muffins

Follow these steps to bake delicious muffins:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C and line a muffin tray with paper liners.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Mix in the vanilla and egg whites.
  4. Stir in the buttermilk.
  5. Combine the flour, baking powder, cocoa, and salt in a bowl.
  6. Slowly add the dry mix to the wet mix.
  7. Fill the muffin liners about 3/4 full with batter.
  8. Bake for 20–25 minutes. If a toothpick comes out clean, they’re done.
  9. Cool for 5 minutes, then move to a rack to cool completely.
